📦 Stock Ordering Forecast

Reorder timing, cash requirements & profit projections — driven by your last 30 days of live sales data.
How cash availability is calculated: When a unit sells, the cash returning to your account = Net Profit per unit + Product & Shipping Cost per unit (because your selling price already recovers the cost of goods). So the total cash available to fund new stock orders = (Net Profit/unit + Unit Cost/unit) × units sold during lead time. This is averaged over the last 30 days per product and applied to the lead time period to determine how much of each order can be self-funded from ongoing sales.

💰 Cash Requirements & Cash Available from Ongoing Sales

Cash Available = (Net Profit/unit + Product & Shipping Cost/unit) × daily sales × lead time days. This is the cash your Amazon account accumulates during the lead time — it includes your profit and the recovery of the unit cost embedded in each sale.
