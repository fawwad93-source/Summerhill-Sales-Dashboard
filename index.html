Profit vs Stock Investment — Chart

Green bars = profit earned during lead time. Red bars = stock purchase cost. Net position is the difference.
How this works: The reorder point = minimum stock buffer + daily sales × lead time. When your FBA stock hits the reorder point, you place an order. It arrives lead-time days later when stock has fallen to exactly the minimum buffer — no disruption. Order quantity refills one full lead-time cycle. Unit cost already includes manufacturing + shipping per unit.

ZCPM2 note: Latest stock is showing 0 — this product is out of stock. Profit projections assume stock is available; actual profit until restocked will be $0.
